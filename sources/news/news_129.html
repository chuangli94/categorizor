VIDEO: UPS driver kicks service dog, pushes owner
Surveillance video provided by Cal Coast News shows a UPS driver kicking a service dog and then pushing the animal's owner outside of an assisted-living facility in Atascadero in May.
By ABC7.com staff
Wednesday, July 08, 2015 09:43PM
ATASCADERO, Calif. (KABC) --
Surveillance video provided by Cal Coast News shows a UPS driver kicking a service dog and then pushing the animal's owner outside of an assisted-living facility in Atascadero in May.

A patient reportedly saw the incident and alerted the dog's handler, Timothy Paulsen, who then approached the UPS driver.

The UPS driver pushed and shoved Paulsen, allegedly causing him to break a rib.

"This assault was completely unprovoked. I nor my dogs did anything to deserve this attack. The driver threatened to kick me as I tried to calm the situation down," Paulsen said.

UPS did not respond to Eyewitness News' request for comment.
